m01

m01 is a new DIY printer designed for studios and creative practices.

The goal is to create an inexpensive printer that removes the frustration and limitation that DIY printers usually entail.

  • 300^3 mm build area
  • Auto bed leveling
  • Wifi printing and printer control
  • Dual Extrusion (with single print head)
  • Heated Bed
  • e3d hotend with up to 1.2mm bore extrusion (fast large prints)
  • 80mm/s print speeds
